Our Mission

Our mission at Flex Flight is to unlock your travel potential. No longer will your lack of financial means prevent you from visiting the places of your dreams. We promise to offer the best flexibility in airfare and will never make comprimises that hurt our customers.

What We Do

FlexFlight is an air travel agency that places the power of air travel in your hands. We offer airfare from all of the major airlines, but without the crippling up-front costs. We pay the airlines up-front, so you don't have to. We offer many different options to pay for your ticket(s) over time. And to top it all off... if life happens and you need to cancel your tickets, recieve a refund, and be on your way.

Forget about the stress of buying expensive airfare and instead focus on the friends and family you want to join for the ride.

How It Works

Find your flight

Just like you are used to with other travel sites. We aggregate all of the best ticket prices from all across the internet for your convenience. Find the best one that fits you and your schedule.

Choose how to Pay

Decide how quickly or slowly you want to pay in the time leading up to your flight. Unlike traditional airfare that requires immediate cash or credit, we will purchase tickets for you up-front.

Want to pay in 12 small payments? No problem. Want to get it over with in 3? More power to you! You decide.

Sit back and relax

Finally, sit back and relax in anticipation of your trip. We will take care of autodrafting month-to-month according to how you chose to pay.

If your plans change... No problem! Cancel your tickets anytime and get your money back.
